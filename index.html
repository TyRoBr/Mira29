Model Formula

Complete mathematical definition of the MIRA-29 scoring model

Variables: LF = Low Flow (minutes) · SD = Shock Delivery (number of Shock) · ED = Epinephrine Dose (mg) · CAS = Cardiac Arrest Site (0 = Street, 1 = Public Facilities, 2 = Home).
MIRA-29 Score = ScoreGood / (ScoreGood + ScoreBad) — interpreted as the probability of neurological outcome. A score < 0.35 indicates Poor Prognosis · a score between 0.35 and 0.75 indicates Intermediate Prognosis · a score > 0.75 indicates Good Prognosis.

MIRA-29 Score

MIRA-29 Score = ScoreGood / ( ScoreGood + ScoreBad )

Interpretation:
  MIRA-29 Score <  0.35              →  Poor prognosis
  0.35 ≤ MIRA-29 Score ≤ 0.75  →  Intermediate prognosis
  MIRA-29 Score >  0.75              →  Good prognosis
